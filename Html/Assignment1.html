Welcome to Abesec

ABES Engineering College (ABESEC), located in Ghaziabad, Uttar Pradesh, is one of the most reputed private institutions affiliated to Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam Technical University (AKTU), Lucknow. Established in the year 2000, the college has steadily built its reputation through strong academics, state-of-the-art infrastructure, industry tie-ups, and an excellent placement record. Over the years, ABES has been recognized by national bodies such as AICTE and UGC, and has also earned an ‘A’ grade accreditation from NAAC. With its continuous performance, the college has been ranked in the 201–300 band of NIRF 2024 under engineering institutions. The sprawling campus spreads across nearly 17 acres, filled with greenery, academic blocks, and modern facilities that create a holistic learning environment for thousands of students. The infrastructure includes dedicated academic buildings named after eminent personalities like Kalpana Chawla, Ramanujan, Aryabhatta, and Bhabha, with each block serving specific departments. The central library is one of the biggest highlights, housing over 80,000 books, 3,800 titles, 16,000+ international journals, digital resources, and video lectures, making it a hub of learning and research. Hostel facilities for both boys and girls are available inside the campus, offering safe, comfortable, and well-equipped living conditions along with mess, laundry, and recreation areas. For extracurricular activities, the college provides extensive sports infrastructure including football, cricket, basketball, and badminton courts
